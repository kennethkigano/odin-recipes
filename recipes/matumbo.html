Matumbo

description

Githeri is a traditional kikuyu dish mainly found the slope of mount kenya.
Its made up mainly of corn and beans boiled together and the some other concoctions are added
a little chanting then the native proceed to gobble

ingredients

  • Maize
  • Beans
  • Waru/potatoes
  • Maai/Water

Steps

  1. Boil maize and beans
  2. Add waru
  3. Add kafisshi
  4. add more water
